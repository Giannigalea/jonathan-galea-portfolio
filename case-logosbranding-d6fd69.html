Atelier 10

Client

Atelier 10

Role

Graphic Designer

Year

2021

The Challenge

Design a sophisticated, artistic logo for a creative studio that focus on Intaglio printing and Lithography.

The Process

Created two variations of an abstract “A” mark built from flowing, interwoven lines incorporating the handwheel.

The Solution

Elegant black and white monogram-style logos that feel both modern and artistic.

Results / Impact

The logo was successfully adopted by Atelier 10 as their official brand mark. Its clean yet detailed design effectively communicates the studio’s creative expertise and has been used consistently across their stationery, digital presence, and promotional materials, helping to establish a strong and memorable visual identity for the studio.
