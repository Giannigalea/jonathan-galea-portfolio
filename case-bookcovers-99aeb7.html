Intermediate Pure Mathematics – Second Edition

Client

Cettina Gauci & Kevin Asciak - University of Malta

Role

Art Director, Graphic Designer, Cover Illustrator & Interior Layout Designer

Year

2001

The Challenge

Design an engaging yet academic cover for an intermediate-level pure mathematics textbook aimed at students preparing for MATSEC or equivalent UK IGCSE examinations. The cover needed to feel modern, approachable, and visually represent mathematical depth and precision without being intimidating.

The Process

I was responsible for the entire artistic direction of the book. This included developing the cover concept from initial ideation through multiple iterations, creating the hypnotic red and orange vortex illustration, refining typography hierarchy, and designing the full interior layout. Explored mathematical motifs such as spirals, fractals, and geometric patterns to symbolize “diving deeper” into complex topics while maintaining clarity and academic credibility.

The Solution

A dynamic swirling vortex design that draws the viewer’s eye inward, paired with strong, clean typography on the cover. The interior was designed with clear visual hierarchy, consistent layout system, and student-friendly formatting to support learning.

Results / Impact

Successfully used as the official cover and interior design for the Second Edition. The comprehensive design approach helped make the textbook more visually appealing and easier to navigate for students while maintaining a professional educational standard.
