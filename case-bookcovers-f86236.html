On Becoming an Effective Teacher

Client

Christopher Bezzina / University of Malta

Role

Art Director, Graphic Designer, Cover Illustrator & Interior Layout Designer

Year

2001

The Challenge

Create a warm, inspiring cover for a handbook aimed at new and aspiring teachers, conveying classroom management, professional growth, and the human side of teaching.

The Process

Focused on symbolic imagery of teaching and learning. Combined photographic elements with vibrant color overlays and bold typography to reflect energy and dedication in education.

The Solution

A colorful, engaging cover featuring classroom scenes with strong title treatment in red and yellow, creating an inviting and motivational feel.

Results / Impact

Well-received by educators and used as the primary cover for the introductory handbook.
