Spatial Narratives

Client

Modern Art Gallery

Role

Exhibition Designer

Year

2024

The Challenge

This exhibition project bridged 2D graphic design with 3D spatial curation. It required designing the environmental graphics, wayfinding, and promotional materials for a contemporary art installation spanning 5 rooms.

The Process

Translating 2D visual elements into a 3D space involved creating physical mockups and digital renders of the gallery space. A significant portion of development was dedicated to testing wayfinding legibility from multiple sightlines within the venue.

(Process imagery placeholders)

The Solution

The spatial design focuses on minimal intervention, using large-scale typographic projections and subtle floor decals to guide visitors. The printed matter mirrors the spatial logic, using inventive folding techniques that reveal information dynamically.

Results / Impact

The seamless integration of wayfinding with the exhibition theme created a highly immersive experience, resulting in record-breaking opening week attendances and wide coverage in design press.
