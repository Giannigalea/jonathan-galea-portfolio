FinanceMalta – 8th Annual Conference Sponsorship Packages

Client

FinanceMalta

Role

Art Director, Graphic Designer, Cover Illustrator & Interior Layout Designer

Year

2015

The Challenge

Design a sophisticated sponsorship brochure for FinanceMalta’s 8th Annual Conference. The document needed to clearly present different sponsorship packages, highlight the benefits of partnering with Malta’s leading financial services industry body, and appeal to potential sponsors from the banking, insurance, investment, and professional services sectors.

The Process

I developed a strong, modern layout using FinanceMalta’s corporate colors (red, white, and grey). The design features a bold 3D isometric graphic of stacked blocks on the cover to symbolize growth, stability, and “building success”. Inside, I created clear, well-organized spreads detailing sponsorship levels, benefits, and opportunities, supported by strong typography and clean visual hierarchy.

The Solution

A professional, high-impact sponsorship package brochure with a striking cover design and clearly structured content. The layout effectively communicates the prestige of the conference and the tangible value of sponsorship opportunities.

Results / Impact

The brochure was successfully used to promote sponsorships for the 8th Annual Conference held at the Hilton Malta. It helped FinanceMalta secure high-profile sponsors and contributed to the overall professional image of the event in Malta’s financial services industry.
