StampaStampa – Team Print Proposal

Client

Atelier 10

Role

Designer

Year

2017

The Challenge

Design a compelling proposal document and supporting materials to promote a specialized team printmaking workshop. The materials needed to clearly explain the workshop’s unique collaborative process, emphasize learning through practice, and appeal to both emerging and experienced printmakers.

The Process

I led the full creative direction for the proposal. This included designing the cover with a strong typographic treatment and bracket motif, creating the interior layout, and developing clear sections that explain the workshop methodology from linocut and lino printing techniques to improvisation, documentation, feedback, and team collaboration. I incorporated candid workshop photography to bring the experience to life.

The Solution

A clean, modern proposal document with a distinctive orange and white color scheme, bold typography, and well-structured content. The design effectively communicates the hands-on, collaborative, and educational nature of the StampaStampa workshop through thoughtful layout, hierarchy, and visual storytelling.

Results / Impact

The proposal was used to successfully promote and recruit participants for the Stampa Stampa printmaking workshops. The professional and engaging design helped convey the quality and unique value of the program, contributing to positive participant feedback and stronger workshop attendance.
