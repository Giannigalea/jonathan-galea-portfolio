Totally Tarantino - Concert

Client

JOBA Events

Role

Poster Designer & Event Visual Designer

Year

2010

The Challenge

Design a bold, high-impact promotional poster for a live music event featuring Tarantino-inspired themes and a strong rock/alt vibe. The poster needed to stand out in a crowded event scene, capture the edgy energy of the night, and clearly communicate event details while appealing to a young adult audience.

The Process

I created a striking, high-contrast design using a stylized black-and-white portrait of a male figure with intense red accents. The composition draws heavy inspiration from Quentin Tarantino’s iconic movie posters with dramatic cropping, bold typography, and strong graphic elements. I carefully balanced the large portrait with event information, logos, and call-to-action details.

The Solution

A powerful red, black, and white poster featuring Tarantino's close-up stylized face, aggressive typography for “TOTALLY TARANTINO”, and clear event information. The design successfully channels a cinematic, rebellious, and energetic mood that perfectly matches the live music event.

Results / Impact

The poster effectively promoted the event held on 6th August at the Greek Theatre, Targa Gap Mosta. It contributed to strong visual branding for JOBA Events and helped create buzz around the Coca-Cola Summer Gigs series.
