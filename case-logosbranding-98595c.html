Dimara Jewellery

Client

Dimara Jewellery

Role

Graphic Designer

Year

2008

The Challenge

Create an elegant, luxurious, and memorable logo for Dimara, a high-end jewellery brand. The mark needed to convey sophistication, craftsmanship, and timeless beauty while being versatile enough for use on packaging, signage, business cards, and digital platforms.

The Process

Focused on premium jewellery aesthetics — clean lines, refined details, and a sense of exclusivity. Developed a custom wordmark with a stylized, graceful “D” incorporating subtle jewellery-inspired elements (delicate curves and refined serifs). Carefully balanced elegance with strong readability.

The Solution

A sophisticated black logo featuring a custom elegant wordmark with a distinctive “D” that evokes luxury and craftsmanship. The design uses refined typography and subtle detailing to create a premium feel that stands out in the competitive jewellery market.

Results / Impact

The logo was successfully implemented as Dimara Jewellery’s official brand identity. Its luxurious and timeless quality has helped strengthen the brand’s visual presence across physical stores, packaging, and marketing materials, effectively communicating the brand’s high-end positioning.
