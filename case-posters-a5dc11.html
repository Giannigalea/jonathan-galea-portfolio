Tactile – 12 Concealed Sculptures

Client

University of Malta / Valletta Design Cluster / EU Japan Fest

Role

Poster & Exhibition Designer

Year

2018

The Challenge

Create a striking poster and visual identity for an exhibition of collaborative concealed sculptures that emphasizes touch, form, and the invisible.

The Process

Developed a unique organic logo mark shaped like a droplet/teardrop containing the word “tactile”. Applied it consistently across poster and related materials.

The Solution

Minimal black poster with a large, elegant white logo and clean typography, evoking mystery and tactility.

Results / Impact

Became the main visual for the exhibition held at the University of Malta (5 Oct – 5 Nov 2018).
