Smoochie – Fresh Fruit Drinks

Client

Smoochie

Role

Website Designer

Year

2013

The Challenge

Design a vibrant, appetizing, and user-friendly website for Smoochie, a fresh fruit drinks brand. The site needed to highlight the natural, healthy, and tempting qualities of the products while making it easy for customers to learn about the brand, browse drinks, and make purchases.

The Process

I focused on creating a fresh and dynamic visual experience using high-quality, mouth-watering product photography. The design features a clean navigation structure with sections such as “Stuff We Make”, “Get to Know Us”, “Fresh & Good”, and “News & Fun”. Special attention was given to responsive design, ensuring the site looks great on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

The Solution

A colorful and energetic website with bold photography of fresh raspberries and fruit drinks, playful yet clean typography, and an appetizing layout. The design successfully captures the fresh, healthy, and fun personality of the Smoochie brand while providing a smooth user experience across all devices.

Results / Impact

The website effectively showcases Smoochie’s product range and brand personality online. It helps attract customers with its vibrant visuals and supports the brand’s goal of presenting fresh fruit temptations “in a bottle”.
