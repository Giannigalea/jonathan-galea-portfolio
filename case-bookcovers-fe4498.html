Staying Ahead: Due Diligence in Residence and Citizenship by Investment Programmes

Client

Investment Migration Council

Role

Art Director, Graphic Designer, Cover Illustrator & Interior Layout Designer

Year

2017

The Challenge

Design a professional, trustworthy cover for a serious publication on investment migration and due diligence, appealing to high-level professionals and governments.

The Process

Used aviation imagery (red airplanes) as a metaphor for “staying ahead” and global mobility. Combined clean typography with a modern blue palette.

The Solution

Striking composition of red airplanes in formation against a clear sky, paired with bold sans-serif typography.

Results / Impact

Created a memorable and relevant visual identity for the publication in the investment migration field.
