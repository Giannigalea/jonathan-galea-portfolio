Movver

Client

Movver Logistics

Role

Brand Identity Designer

Year

2016

The Challenge

Create a modern, dynamic logo and supporting brand elements for a global logistics and transportation company that conveys movement, precision, technology, and connectivity.

The Process

Extensive concept exploration (“Concept Metamorphosis”). Developed multiple iterations of an abstract geometric mark combining letter “M”, directional arrows, and diamond shapes. Tested various color variations and layout options.

The Solution

A clean, abstract logo mark suggesting forward motion, paired with a modern sans-serif wordmark and comprehensive usage guidelines.

Results / Impact

Delivered a complete, flexible brand identity suitable for both digital and print applications.
