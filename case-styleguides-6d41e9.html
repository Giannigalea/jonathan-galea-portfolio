Movver – Brand Style Guidelines

Client

Movver Logistics

Role

Art Director & Brand Guidelines Designer

Year

2016

The Challenge

Following the creation of the new Movver logo, develop a comprehensive set of brand style guidelines to ensure consistent and professional application of the visual identity across all touchpoints. The guidelines needed to clearly define logo usage, color variations, typography, layouts, and applications for a global logistics company.

The Process

I designed a detailed brand bible that expanded on the earlier logo development (“Concept Metamorphosis”). This included documenting primary and secondary logo versions, color specifications (CMYK, RGB, Pantone), correct and incorrect usage rules, typography hierarchy (including sans-serif options), horizontal and vertical layouts, and various real-world applications.

The Solution

A clean, well-structured brand guidelines document featuring multiple logo lockups, precise color palettes, detailed do’s and don’ts, and practical examples of usage across stationery, marketing materials, and digital applications. The design maintains a modern, dynamic, and professional tone suitable for the logistics sector.

Results / Impact

The Movver Brand Style Guidelines provided the client with a clear, professional reference tool that ensures visual consistency across all future communications. This helped strengthen the Movver brand identity and supported the company’s goal of presenting a unified, modern image in a competitive global market.
