Life Drawing Models

Client

Malta Society of Arts (MSA)

Role

Graphic Designer

Year

2023

The Challenge

Design an eye-catching poster to recruit life drawing models for weekly art classes, clearly communicating practical information while appealing to artists and potential models.

The Process

Used a simple, clean composition featuring a wooden stool as the central visual element. Combined bold red “APPLY NOW” call-to-action with clear body text.

The Solution

Minimal yet effective poster with strong hierarchy, ample white space, and a friendly, inviting tone.

Results / Impact

Successfully promoted the life drawing sessions organized by the Malta Society of Arts.
